How It Works

From question to answer
in under two seconds.

InSight's dual-eye system splits the cognitive load — your left eye stays on track, your right eye stays informed.

01
🎙
InSight listens
Dual microphones in the temple arms capture incoming speech, isolating questions from ambient noise in real time.
02
🧠
AI generates options
Claude AI analyzes the question, pulls live web data, and generates 2–4 response strategies with facts and anticipated rebuttals.
03
👁
You select by gaze
Response cards appear on the right lens. Dwell one second, blink, or tap the remote to confirm your choice.
04
📜
Left lens activates
Your response loads as a live teleprompter. The opening line appears first, then words follow naturally at your pace.

📚 Scholar Mode

Research while you write.
Write before you research.

Scholar Mode turns InSight into an active research partner — connecting ideas, surfacing sources, and building citations as you work through any document or writing project.

🔗
Idea Connection
As you read or write, InSight surfaces connections between concepts across your open documents, suggesting how ideas relate or conflict.
✂
Smart Quote Clipping
Look at a passage and say "clip that" — InSight captures the quote with full citation metadata: author, title, publisher, page, date, and format.
🔍
Write First, Source Later
Draft your argument freely — InSight's agent scans academic databases, archives, and the web to find supporting sources and suggest where they integrate.
📋
Auto-Formatted Citations
Every clip and suggestion arrives citation-ready in MLA, APA, Chicago, or Bluebook — appropriate for academic, journalism, or legal work.

🔧 Field Mode

Instant intelligence
on everything you see.

Field Mode turns InSight into a live assessment tool — giving sales professionals, insurance adjusters, mechanics, and retail staff instant, actionable intelligence on any product or person in front of them.

🏍
Vehicle & Unit Analysis
Look at a vehicle — InSight pulls specs, current market value, trade-in range, recall history, and known issues for that make, model, and year instantly.
🔍
Damage & Defect Flagging
For insurance adjusters and mechanics, the forward camera flags visible damage, cross-references common failure points, and estimates repair cost ranges.
👗
Retail Style Assistant
In fashion retail, InSight reads the customer's current aesthetic and stated preferences, then suggests looks, checks live inventory, and bridges to online alternatives.
📦
Live Inventory Integration
Connected to your store system, InSight shows stock levels, size availability, and reorder status in real time — without leaving the customer's side.
