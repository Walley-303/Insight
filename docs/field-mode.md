# Field Mode

Field Mode is InSight's on-the-ground professional intelligence layer. It overlays real-time data on physical environments — vehicles, buildings, people, and neighborhoods.

## Visual Identity

- Color: Orange (`#f97316`)
- Corner brackets: Orange-tinted
- Background: Deep warm-dark gradient

## Scenarios

### 1. Vehicle Scan
**Use case:** Motorcycle / auto dealership sales floor

- Left lens detects and identifies the vehicle with an AR bounding box
  - Example: "2019 Harley-Davidson Road Glide Special — VIN confirmed"
- Right lens surfaces:
  - **Vehicle Intelligence card** — market value range, trade-in range, mileage status, open recalls, known issues, title status
  - **Suggested Talking Points** — AI-generated negotiation and sales guidance based on the vehicle's data

### 2. Damage Flag
**Use case:** Insurance assessment or vehicle inspection

- Left lens overlays AR bounding boxes on detected damage zones with severity labels (HIGH / MODERATE / INSPECT)
- Right lens surfaces:
  - **Front Panel — High Severity** — damage type, estimated repair cost ($4,200–$6,800), structural risk flag
  - **Side Panel — Moderate** — damage type, repair cost, paint match warning
  - **Total Estimate** — combined repair range, claim threshold flag, recommendation

### 3. Style Assist
**Use case:** Retail floor associate helping a customer

- Left lens identifies the customer's style profile (e.g., "Casual-chic · Earth tones · Minimalist")
- Right lens surfaces:
  - **Recommended Look** — specific items (top, bottom, footwear, accessory) with location in store
  - **Live Inventory** — real-time stock levels by size, with online alternatives if out of stock
  - **Upsell** — complementary item suggestion with average basket impact

### 4. KC Field Intel
**Use case:** Entrepreneur or analyst walking a neighborhood for site selection

- Left lens overlays AR zone labels on the streetscape (Opportunity Zone, High Foot Traffic)
- Right lens surfaces:
  - **Waldo / Brookside card** — population trend, specialty food gap, Yelp signal, competitor proximity, opportunity score (87/100)
  - **Crossroads / Midtown card** — median age, supply skew, resident gap, redevelopment activity, opportunity score (79/100)
  - **InSight Recommendation** — plain-language synthesis of field data vs. dashboard analysis

## Kansas City Focus

The KC Field Intel scenario is tailored to Kansas City neighborhoods, demonstrating how InSight can be localized to specific markets for real estate, F&B, and retail site selection.
