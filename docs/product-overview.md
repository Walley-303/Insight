# InSight — Product Overview

InSight is an AI-powered smart glasses platform that overlays real-time intelligence onto the user's field of view through a dual-lens HUD interface.

## Core Concept

Two lenses, two roles:
- **Left lens** — Document / field view (what the user is looking at)
- **Right lens** — AI intelligence panel (contextual information, suggestions, and actions)

## Operating Modes

### Scholar Mode
Designed for researchers, students, and academics. InSight assists with reading, writing, and research tasks in real time.

### Field Mode
Designed for professionals operating in physical environments — dealerships, retail floors, insurance assessments, and urban market analysis.

## Key Differentiators

- Gaze-aware document interaction (dwell to clip, annotate, or act)
- Live AI intelligence without breaking the user's physical workflow
- Dual-lens split means the world view is never obscured by the data overlay
- Remote control support for hands-free navigation between scenarios
